Mentor Portfolios


Robert Cory Molloy

Cory was a member of his high school FRC team and was a mentor for another team during his years in college. He joined team 2659 in the summer of 2017 and has led the team to a new level of competitiveness while also starting a successful career at Universal Studios. Currently Cory is helping the team improve their newly acquired workshop while also introducing the team to off season competitions. He shows students how to be more organized and use technology to benefit the team.


Brian Gobeli Sr.

Brian was a parent volunteer during the 2016 and 2017 season, he loved the program so much that he chose to continue to volunteer after his son graduated from the school in 2017. He is the leader in all things mechanical, he plans to teach students how to operate all the necessary machinery to have a safe and successful workshop. Brian is currently working on getting the newly acquired workshop equipped with all the necessary machinery.


Kevin O'Shea

Mr. O’shea started team 2659 back in 2008 with the help of several students and a couple parent volunteers. Mr. O’shea leads the management team while also being the team’s leader in moral. He is the teacher in charge of the program and is currently working with a group of students to acquire more sponsors.

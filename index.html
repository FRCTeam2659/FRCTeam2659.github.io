WHO WE ARE

The RoboWarriors is a robotics team at Bishop Alemany High School in Mission Hills, California. Since 2008, our rookie year, we have had the opportunity to compete in FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) with other schools and robotic's teams. Over the years, our goal has been to spread the message of FIRST and STEM which is to inspire and help students learn more about Science and Technology.

OUR SIX-WEEK JOURNEY

Bishop Alemany High School's Robotics team is unlike the other teams that are going to be competing in the 2017 FIRST Robotics Competition. To learn more about our enjoyable six week journey, feel free to click the button below!

PROJECTS

Every year we have six weeks to build and design a working robot. The team also build projects during off-season. All of us grow closer together during this process. To be updated on upcoming and present projects visit the link below.

COMPETITION

Combing the excitement of sport with the rigors of science and technology. We call FIRST Robotics Competition the ultimate Sport for the Mind. High school student participants call it "the hardest fun you'll ever have." Each season ends with an exciting FIRST Championship.

DONATIONS

Every team is given a certain budget on supplies for their own robot. If you want to help us out, we would appreciate any donation amount. To donate towards the future of the Bishop Alemany Robotics Team, click the button below.
